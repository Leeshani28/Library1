- Book of the month -

The mistress. A mysterious message. A chance to get it right. Stephanie Laredo is devastated when the man she loves returns to his wife and her only daughter moves across the country to live with her father. Suddenly, Stephanie finds herself alone for the first time in her life. Confronted by her father’s declining health and one too many hangovers, Stephanie cannot deny she has made a habit of making poor decisions and something needs to change. When a mysterious message piques her interest, Stephanie must face the painful past she’s done her best to forget. With the help of her oldest friend, a pup named Scout and a handsome wine maker named Ben--Stephanie begins to remember who she was and who she wants to be.
