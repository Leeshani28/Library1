Beautiful Editions

If you want to start book collecting, here are my favourite editions. These books can be more expensive than their mass market paperback alternatives, but keep them well and they will retain their value.Some of them are, Canterbury Classics Word Cloud, Virago Modern Classics, Vintage Russian Classics, Thomas Nelson Seasons Editions, Penguin English Library, Knickerbocker Classics, Chiltern Classics, Macmillan Collector's Library.


Children's

Publishers and people in the industry use the terms “Picturebooks” for Picture Storybooks as well, so it is up to you to understand what they mean. If they are referring to a children's book longer than 900 words, they mean a Picture Storybook.You can also access over 500 children's books via the lancaster library.


Computers & Technology

Books in the computer nonfiction genre are about the technology, application, function, and maintenance of computers. The books in this genre are designed to help people learn and understand the function and operation of a computer.You can access computer & technology books since beginner level upto advance level via our library.

Subjects & research guides

Aimed at anyone new to the Lancaster Libraries, our subject and research guides will help you identify key library resources and tools for your studies and research. Our subject librarians provide guides to the latest and most useful library resources in your discipline.
